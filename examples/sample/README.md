## Historical Facts

America was founded in 1776 when the thirteen American colonies declared their independence from Great Britain. The Declaration of Independence was signed on July 4, 1776, a date that is now celebrated as Independence Day in the United States.

## Key Figures

Some of the founding fathers include:
- George Washington
- Thomas Jefferson
- Benjamin Franklin
- John Adams
- James Madison

## The Constitution

The United States Constitution was written in 1787 and ratified in 1788. It established the framework for the federal government and is considered one of the most important documents in American history.

## Bill of Rights

The first ten amendments to the Constitution, known as the Bill of Rights, were ratified in 1791. These amendments guarantee fundamental rights and liberties to all American citizens, including freedom of speech, religion, and the press.

## Expansion

Throughout the 19th century, the United States expanded westward through purchases, treaties, and conflicts. The Louisiana Purchase in 1803 doubled the size of the nation, and subsequent acquisitions added vast territories to the growing country.
